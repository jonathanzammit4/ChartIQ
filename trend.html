📈 Trend & Moving Averages

Tools that smooth price noise to reveal the underlying direction of a market. This is the foundation of all technical analysis — start here if you're new to trading.

Trend ★☆☆ Easy

SMA

// Simple Moving Average

Smooths price noise by averaging closing prices over N periods. The foundation of all trend-following strategies.
